What is Pure Text Field?

A minimal tool that strips all formatting from any text you paste into it — fonts, sizes, colors, links, bold, italic — leaving you with clean, plain text ready to use anywhere.

Your content is saved automatically in your browser's local storage and restored on refresh. Each browser tab is fully independent with its own content. No database, no server-side processing — nothing you type ever leaves your device.
